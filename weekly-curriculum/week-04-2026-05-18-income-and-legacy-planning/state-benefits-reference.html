States With No Income Tax

These 9 states do not tax any personal income, which means all retirement income — military pension, Social Security, TSP withdrawals, and investment gains — is state-tax-free.

Alaska Florida Nevada New Hampshire* South Dakota Tennessee Texas Washington Wyoming

Property Tax Exemptions for Veterans

  • All 50 states offer some form of property tax relief for veterans
  • Most require a VA disability rating (often 100% for full exemption)
  • Some states extend partial exemptions to veterans rated 10%+
  • Surviving spouses often qualify but must apply separately
  • Contact your county assessor's office or state DVA to apply

How to Find Your State Benefits

  • va.gov/statedva.htm — State DVA directory (find your state's Department of Veterans Affairs)
  • ncsl.org — National Conference of State Legislatures (state tax policy research)
  • Your local VSO — DAV, VFW, and American Legion posts can help you navigate state-specific benefits
