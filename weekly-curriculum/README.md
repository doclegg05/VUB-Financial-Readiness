# VUB Financial Readiness Course - 6-Week Curriculum

This folder organizes the course into the six Monday meetings scheduled for Spring 2026.

## Class Calendar

| Week | Date | Class Plan |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Monday, April 27, 2026 | Pre-test + Module 1: Retirement Timelines & Preparation |
| 2 | Monday, May 4, 2026 | Module 2: Disability Pay & Compensation |
| 3 | Monday, May 11, 2026 | Module 3: Healthcare & Long-Term Care |
| 4 | Monday, May 18, 2026 | Module 4 + condensed Module 5: Income, legacy, and estate planning |
| 5 | Monday, May 25, 2026 | No class - Memorial Day |
| 6 | Monday, June 1, 2026 | Review + post-test |

## Teaching Notes

- Use `index.html` at the project root as the live classroom course.
- The files in each weekly folder are copied from the master teaching folders so the original web app links keep working.
- Week 4 combines the final content week into the May 18 class because May 25 is reserved for Memorial Day.
- Week 6 should stay focused on review, questions, post-test completion, and next-step resources.
- Videos are not duplicated into these folders because they are large. Use `videos/output/` for the finished MP4s.
- Use `GOOGLE-DRIVE-COLLECTION.md` to prepare the assessment upload folders and student return workflow.
