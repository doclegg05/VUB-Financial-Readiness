CHAMPVA — Dependent Coverage

For spouses and dependents of veterans who are permanently and totally disabled (or who died from service-connected conditions).

  • Covers 75% of allowable charges
  • $50/person annual deductible
  • $3,000 annual catastrophic cap
  • No enrollment fee or monthly premium

Aid & Attendance Eligibility Requirements

Service Requirements

  • 90+ days active duty service
  • At least 1 day during a wartime period
  • Discharge other than dishonorable
  • Age 65+ or permanently disabled

Daily Help to Document

  • Bathing — washing & grooming
  • Mobility — transferring & walking
  • Dressing — putting on clothing
  • Toileting — using the restroom
  • Eating — feeding oneself
  • Supervision — safety, memory, or hazards

Note: Rates shown are rounded monthly maximums based on 2026 MAPR tables. Actual payment is reduced by countable income after allowable deductions.

⚠

Medicaid Warning: If you receive Medicaid-funded nursing home care, Aid & Attendance benefits are reduced to approximately $90/month. Plan accordingly and consult a VA-accredited claims agent before applying.

📅
Key Deadline
Enroll in Medicare Parts A & B at age 65. Missing your Initial Enrollment Period results in permanently higher premiums for the rest of your life.
