How To Return Your Assessment Results

VUB Financial Readiness Course
Google Drive Upload Link
https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1VCfwJXmYgKexesffLbW34FlQd1d86gth?usp=drive_link

Steps

  1. Complete the pre-test or post-test in your browser.
  2. On the final results screen, click Download Results (CSV).
  3. Find the downloaded CSV file in your Downloads folder.
  4. Open the Google Drive upload link above.
  5. Upload your CSV file.
  6. Tell the instructor when your file has uploaded.
Your file name should look similar to vub-pre-test-last-name-2026-04-27.csv or vub-post-test-last-name-2026-06-01.csv.
If upload does not work: email the CSV file to your instructor or ask for help before leaving class.
